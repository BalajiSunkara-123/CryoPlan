# CryoPlan Future Algorithms Repository

This workspace is reserved for complex spatial planning engines:
- **LSE_Safety_Check**: Computes slope steepness, lighting angles, and lines of sight to confirm landing zones.
- **Pragyan_A_Star**: An offline route planner that optimizes traversal energy cost, wheel slippage, and thermal exposure.
- **DFSAR_Ice_Classifier**: Performs multi-frequency polarimetric decompositions to detect subsurface volatile reserves.
